MARKETVIBE SALES NAVIGATOR COMPANION - START HERE

1. Open Microsoft Edge.
2. Go to:
   edge://extensions
3. Turn on Developer mode.
4. Click Load unpacked.
5. Select this FOLDER, not a file:
   C:\marketvibe-pro\browser-extension\sales-navigator-companion
6. Confirm you see:
   MarketVibe Sales Navigator Companion

DAILY USE

1. Log in to Sales Navigator:
   https://www.linkedin.com/sales/search/people
2. The MarketVibe panel appears bottom-right.
3. Click Start finder.
4. Let it open searches and capture visible qualified result cards.
   The finder targets builders, construction firms, property developers,
   luxury real-estate companies, estate agents, and decision-makers.
   It rejects marketing agencies, lead-generation agencies, SEO agencies,
   advertising agencies, and consultants as lead records.
5. The tool pauses after a conservative batch. Do not force continuous runs.
6. Click Send to MarketVibe.
7. If asked, log in to MarketVibe admin:
   https://www.marketvibe1.com/admin/import
8. The import page will automatically import the captured batch.
9. Download CSV only if you want a backup.
10. Wait before starting another batch.

IMPORTANT

- The tool captures visible result cards only.
- It does not collect cookies, passwords, private messages, or hidden LinkedIn API data.
- If LinkedIn asks for verification, stop and complete it manually.
- If LinkedIn shows Too Many Requests, stop and wait. Do not restart immediately.
- If the panel does not show, refresh the Sales Navigator tab after loading the extension.
